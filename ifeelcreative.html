Creative and tasty additions to your recipe

If you want to make this recipe a little more personal, here are a few tips:

  • You can add dark chocolate chips instead of raisins.
  • The bread can be replaced with dry brioche or croissants, even donuts would do.
  • Cream instead of milk will make the base more rich.
  • A bit of nutmeg on top will make a big difference.
  • Some apple or apricot jam on top of the bread will give it a nice twist.
